JS-Chin

JS-Chin is a game based on one of my favourite board games: Chińczyk! (I believe it's called CHIOOCHOO in english 🤔). The rules are simple: 4 players, 4 pawns each, first with 4 pawns on the finish line wins! I always liked this board game because the it is never over until it's over: everything can happen when you get lucky! Or unlucky for that matter!
